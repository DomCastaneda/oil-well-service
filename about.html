Our Company

Oil Crew Service Inc. delivers dependable field support for oil and gas operations throughout Southern California. Our team works directly with drilling, completion, and production crews to provide practical assistance that keeps projects moving safely and efficiently.

Built on field experience, our company focuses on responsive service, reliable equipment support, and the ability to adapt quickly to the demands of active operations. From drilling support to completion assistance and production maintenance, we understand the pace and conditions of real field work.

Our approach is straightforward: show up prepared, work safely, and deliver dependable results that crews can count on. We take pride in supporting operators and service companies with the equipment, coordination, and field knowledge needed to keep operations running smoothly.

Oil Crew Service Inc. is committed to maintaining strong working relationships with our partners by delivering consistent service, clear communication, and reliable support across every project.
